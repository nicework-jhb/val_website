Welcome to Val Hotel

The almost unknown little hamlet of Val is about 50 minutes from Heidelberg in Gauteng and only a stone's throw from Greylingstad on the road to Standerton. It is only 160km or 90mins from OR Thambo International. It is surely one of the smallest, cleanest and most beautiful little towns on the highveld and with only 28 inhabitants, it is really worth a visit.

Accommodation

Val Hotel has 12 rooms, 6 self-catering cottages and a backpackers which sleeps 15. It can accommodate 60 or more guests and offers full catering.

About Val

An Inn was established that would serve the stagecoach route from the goldfields of the Lowveld to Johannesburg. The first Post Office stamp was dated "Waterval 1888".

Weddings

Val is an amazing venue for your country wedding. Get married in the The Church of St. Francis' of Assisi, and have your reception in the quaint hotel.
