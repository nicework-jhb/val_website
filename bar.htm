Moeggeploegkroeg

This quirky pub was once a part of the original store of Jos Smith and Sons.

Today it offers light meals and an intimate venue for smaller groups up to 50. The pub has been chosen as one of the “101 Best Beloved Bars in Southern Africa” by travel writer Chris Marais.

A favourite for bikers, hikers, writers, (dykers, no just kidding!) music lovers and party animals
